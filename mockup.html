School Zone Map

    Notes on Middle School Zones:

    • Nelson Middle School: Enrollment at Nelson Middle School is at capacity for the campus. Any new students who live in the Nelson zone and enroll for the 2022-23 school year will attend Wester Middle School. Transportation will be provided upon request to Wester. Access more information on busing. To finalize the enrollment process and request transportation, parents of new students should contact Wester Middle School directly.
